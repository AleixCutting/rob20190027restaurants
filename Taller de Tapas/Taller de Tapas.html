CTaller de Tapas

Taller de Tapas has six restaurants spread around Barcelona. If you walk the wide, tree-lined avenue that straddles the centre of the Rambla Catalunya and you’ll arrive to the newest jewel of the Taller de Tapas collection..

It’s a perfect lunch or breakfast spot and a wonderful place to meet and wander in the evening. You can dine al fresco at shady tables on the tree-lined boulevard (just like Scarlett Johanssen and Woody Allen in his latest film)..

They present a gastronomic offer based on traditional tapas and market dishes, always based on highest quality seasonal produce. The restaurant is cozy and also has a private room for groups and a pleasant terrace on the street whose tables are hotly contested from spring.

Location: City center

Dining room capacities: 100 pax

Price Level: Medium

Style: Mediterranean cuisine

Special diets: Available

Handicapped facilities: No
